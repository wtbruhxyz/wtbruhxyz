About

Hello, Welcome to my little corner of the Internet! where curiosity sparks creativity and every story finds its voice. I’m Aldrian Troy Bumalay, a 19 year-old currently living in Antipolo, Rizal. As a student pursuing a degree in Information Technology at Rizal Technological University, I’m passionate about exploring the endless possibilities that technology offers.

Before embarking on this exciting journey, I graduated from Cainta Senior High Schools, where I first discovered my love for coding and digital creativity. Those years were filled with late-night study sessions and collaborative projects that ignited my curiosity and fueled my imagination.

Here, in my little corner, I believe that curiosity sparks creativity and every story deserves to be told. Join me as I share insights, ideas, and a bit of my personal journey where each post is a step in the adventure of learning and growth!
